ABOUT

MYSELF

Hello! I am a Computer Science-Economics and Philosophy concentrator at Brown University from the historical city of Melaka, Malaysia.

Growing up in a low-income single-parent family, I was taught that education was a means to attaining security and comfort in life. It was not until I lived with other 62 nationalities and first studied philosophy at a United World College that I experienced learning as an empowering tool for community-building and bridging multiple truths.

Since then, I have been exploring multiple disciplines, which leads me to the intersection of technology, philosophy, economics and design. I am passionate about using technology to scale community-driven solutions, and always eager to learn a new Python data science library or to play a new Taiko(traditional Japanese drumming) song.

I’m always excited to meet new faces and hear new stories, so please don’t hesitate to reach out.
